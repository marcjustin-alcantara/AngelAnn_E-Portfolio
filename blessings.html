GIVING BLESSINGS

Last Christmas, my heart and my little brother Zaimon's heart led us to Taal, where we hoped to share some blessings with those in need. Our gifts may not have been extravagant, but they were given with a pure heart, offered to the Lord as a token of our deepest gratitude. We wanted to thank Jesus for His constant strength, for His unwavering presence in our lives, and for teaching us to never give up, even when faced with challenges. It was a simple act of giving, but it was filled with the spirit of Christmas, a heartfelt expression of our love and appreciation for all that we have been given.
