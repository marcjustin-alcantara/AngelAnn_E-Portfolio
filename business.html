BUSINESS VENTURES

Beyond the classroom, my heart has led me to explore different paths, each one teaching me invaluable lessons. Each of these experiences, though diverse, has woven together a tapestry of growth, preparing me for the future with a deeper understanding of myself and the world around me.

Independent Hustle (Flower Making)

Since 2025, the delicate art of flower making has blossomed into a small business. I started this because I deeply wanted to experience earning my own money through sheer hard work, without having to ask my family for an allowance. Nurturing my creativity and resourcefulness through this craft taught me the true value of an honestly earned income.

Supporting My Studies (Keychain Business)

Selling keychains became my way of independently supporting my school needs. I focused this small business specifically on paying for class funds, school requirements, and other academic expenses. It became a joyful way to connect with others, all while making sure I could sustain my own educational journey without placing a burden on my parents.

Enhancing My Skills (Tutoring)

I entered the world of tutoring because I actively wanted to explore and enhance my skills when it comes to teaching. Guiding a young student has filled me with a profound sense of purpose. It gave me the opportunity to practice my patience and communication, while having the rewarding experience of witnessing their growth and understanding firsthand.

Survival & Resilience (Tindera at Mac Foods)

My time at Mac Foods was truly a survival job. I experienced a tremendous amount of struggles here—from selling frozen chicken to manually carrying heavy boxes and loading them into the freezer. It was incredibly exhausting work. However, I survived and fostered deep resilience. Through that struggle, I managed to save almost 20,000 to buy my own drums. It opened my eyes to the real world and proved my own strength.
