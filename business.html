BUSINESS VENTURES

Beyond the classroom, my heart has led me to explore different paths, each one teaching me invaluable lessons. Since 2025, the delicate art of flower making has blossomed into a small business, nurturing my creativity and resourcefulness. Selling keychains has become a joyful way to connect with others, sharing little pieces of happiness. Tutoring a young student has filled me with a sense of purpose, witnessing their growth and understanding. Even my time as a 'tindera' at Mac Foods, though challenging, has opened my eyes to the world beyond school, fostering resilience and empathy. Each of these experiences, though diverse, has woven together a tapestry of growth, preparing me for the future with a deeper understanding of myself and the world around me.
