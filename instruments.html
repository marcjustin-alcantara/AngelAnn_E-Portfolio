MELODIC HEALING

Looking back, I realize how fortunate I was to have parents who saw something special in me, even when I was just a child. They playfully called me their little rockstar, and while I may not have become one in the traditional sense, their early encouragement instilled in me a lifelong love for music and self-expression. It was in high school that I discovered the guitar, and it quickly became my constant companion. I spent countless hours learning to play, finding solace and joy in the simple act of creating music. Later, when health challenges arose, including a heart condition that I still manage today, my parents, with their boundless kindness, gifted me a piano. It was intended as a way to pass the time, a gentle distraction, but it became so much more. The drums, however, hold a unique place in my heart. They weren't a gift, but rather something I worked hard to achieve on my own. Saving nearly 20,000 from my job at Mac Foods, I was able to purchase them with my own money. These instruments aren't a symbol of extravagance or entitlement, but rather a testament to my own passion and the unwavering support of my parents, who, with open hearts, allowed me to pursue my dreams and find healing in the power of music. I am deeply grateful for their love, their understanding, and their willingness to let me explore the creative path that has brought so much joy to my life.
