MELODIC HEALING

A lifelong journey of finding solace, joy, and profound self-expression through the power of music.

The Spark (Childhood Rockstar)

Looking back, I realize how fortunate I was to have parents who saw something special in me, even when I was just a child. They playfully called me their little rockstar, and while I may not have become one in the traditional sense, their early encouragement instilled in me a lifelong love for music and self-expression.

Finding Solace (Guitar & Piano)

It was in high school that I discovered the guitar, and it quickly became my constant companion. I spent countless hours learning to play, finding solace and joy in the simple act of creating music. Later, when health challenges arose, including a heart condition that I still manage today, my parents, with their boundless kindness, gifted me a piano. It was intended as a way to pass the time, a gentle distraction, but it became so much more.

Earning My Passion (Self Interest)

The drums, however, hold a unique place in my heart. They weren't a gift, but rather something I worked hard to achieve on my own. Saving nearly ₱20,000 from my job at Mac Foods, I was able to purchase them with my own money. These instruments aren't a symbol of extravagance or entitlement, but rather a testament to my own passion and the unwavering support of my parents, who, with open hearts, allowed me to pursue my dreams and find healing in the power of music. I am deeply grateful for their love, their understanding, and their willingness to let me explore the creative path that has brought so much joy to my life.

Pressure & Triumph (Battle of the Bands)

During the Battle of the Bands, we became champions in our first year. We weren't as fortunate during our second year, but by our third year, we won the championship again! Behind those wins was a lot of unseen struggle and pressure, especially after our loss, because we needed to bounce back for our college department. All we could do was give it our absolute best, perform from the heart, and make our department proud.
